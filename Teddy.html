• Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • Teddy bears and other toys account for twenty two deaths each year, and nearly one hundred and fifty thousand injuries.
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  • She created a soft plush with movable arms and legs and a friendly face after taking her nephew to the zoo in 1903.
  • The name teddy bear comes from former United States President Theodore Roosevelt, who was commonly known as "Teddy" (though he loathed being referred to as such). ... Morris Michtom saw the drawing of Roosevelt and was inspired to create a teddy bear.
